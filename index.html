S1 History Exam Survival Guidelines / 考試奪分策略

💡 Active Recall (主動回憶法)

Don't just read notes repeatedly. Use the flashcards in Revision Unit 5 and test your recall. Attempt the interactive quiz multiple times until you secure a 5/5 score. Let mistakes be your best teacher!

💡 Direct Cause & Effect (釐清因果關係)

History essay questions heavily penalize students who memorize facts but fail to link them. For example: always connect mountainous Greek geography directly to the isolation of communities and the birth of independent city-states.

💡 DBQ Data Analysis (史料分析能力)

In Data-Based Questions (DBQ), analyze worksheets and source images closely. Click the Worksheet Companion tool in the revision slide to cross-reference historical data and sources directly while reading our notes.

💡 Spelling & Vocabulary (核心詞彙拼寫)

Spelling counts! Ensure you can spell key vocabulary correctly (e.g. *Minoan*, *Mycenaean*, *Oligarchy*, *Democracy*, *Byzantine*). Capitalize proper nouns like *Athens*, *Sparta*, and *Alexander*.
